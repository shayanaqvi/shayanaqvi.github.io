Third-Party Youtube Clients

My experience with these (on the desktop) has generally not been great. There's always some kind of problem: the app is too slow, there are problems with video playback and so on and so forth.

While I really dislike its name, FreeTube is the first third-party Youtube client I've used that doesn't have these problems. It's been great so far! Good stuff!
