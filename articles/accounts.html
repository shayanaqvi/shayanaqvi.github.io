Accounts

Accounts for this, for that, for every single thing. Does your thing even need an account? Who cares! Shove accounts down everyone's throat!

Quite literally, this is what accounts have become. You need one for everything. Do you need to download a file from a website you're never going to use again for your entire existence? Create an account!

Mind you, things aren't that bad. Even sites like Youtube still let you use it without an account. We're not far from things getting that bad though. I'm quite sure of it.
