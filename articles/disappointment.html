Disappointment

This definitely does not apply to all people. The disappointment of some people, and you probably know who those people are to you, is an important thing to learn from.

This friend who's "disappointed" you did something? Ask yourself: did you do anything wrong? If no, then ask yourself: is their disappointment worth anything?
