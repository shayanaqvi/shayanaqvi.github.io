Photoshop is not Graphic Design!

Something I see, at least where I live, is people touting their knowledge of graphic design because they know how to use Photoshop & co.

Here’s the thing: Photoshop is not graphic design!

There’s more to graphic design than being able to create graphics. There’s knowledge of colours, fonts, heirarchy, layouts, so on and so forth. Graphic design doesn’t even require a computer!

So, if you know Photoshop, you know Photoshop. Not, automatically, by extension, graphic design.
