Speaking Slowly

It's to do with clarity. I can slow myself down in conversation with someone, but in those extremely short exchanges with someone, where all I need to say is "okay" or "yes" or "no", they turn out problematic.

That's because they sound a bit like grunting. Try saying "okay" really fast, and you'll see where I'm coming from. I wouldn't say it makes a great impression, especially with acquaintances.
