Gaming

I acquired a Playstation Vita back in October/November of last year, I don't think I used it much until about December, when I got back into Minecraft. Yes, the Playstation Vita edition. It's old, but I have a lot of nostalgia for the legacy console editions of the game. It has issues with performance, but it's rather playable.

I've gotten pretty far into NFS Most Wanted too. It's really novel playing that kind of game on something like a Vita. It does have a fair few technical issues though, especially with framerate, and overally I think it's a pretty average game. It's nice for what it is, but it's incredibly repetitive.

Technical issues are kind of a recurring theme for Playstation Vita (and to an extent, Playstation Portable) games. Especially framerates. None of the games I've played thus far with framerate issues are unplayable, so to speak, but they're less than ideal.

Midnight Club L.A. Remix is one of those games. God, the framerate in that game is quite annoying. It's never unplayable, but it's teetering on the edge of that.
