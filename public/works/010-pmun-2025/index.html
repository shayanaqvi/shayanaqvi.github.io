Instagram Posts

The only thing I have against these is that the text is a bit too glowy. But other than that, I like these.

A bit of a side note about the background: everything here uses only one background image made by my co-director. In order to ensure we could reuse this any many times as possible, I would crop the image to a random section, rotate it however looked good and then apply a blur + grain effect to mask the lower resolution. This is EXACTLY the sort of thing I should have done last year for the Nobelium Cup. Nobody we showed our work to seemed to be able to tell that we were doing this! You might be able to tell though if you look closely. Mind you, this wasn’t a perfect solution but I think it was a step in the right direction.

The full background is pretty much what you can see across all of the 9 posts below. Any subsequent backgrounds were obtained in the way I mentioned above.

I tried using this font selection a lot more consistently this time around, and I think it worked out.
