MSEM

As part of a school event (about math/science/arts), I did some design work, mostly for social media, including promotional material and the logo.

This figure was made about a year ago, and some things in this just ain't right. Regardless:

Looking back on this, over a year later, there's one thing lacking: consistency. There's some, but it's not much, and everything looks all over the place. I find it a bit funny because I made a whole point of creating this thing:

The fonts stayed the same, but the colours? They stayed only for the logo! It's just all over the place after that. Some posts have red accents, some green, some purple, some with accents taken from the image. And where did that blue come from on the ID cards of the team members? Why is the background of the director ID cards black?

The concert ticket is pretty ugly too: I can't stand the way I blurred the seams of the photos. Why has Jetbrains Mono been used there!? The margin on the right side is pretty bland too.

A year of hindsight really does something I guess. I remember kind of liking the ticket design.

But putting all of this aside, it's definitely a start. It was my first time doing something as extensive as this for a single project, and it does indeed look like a first time. But all things start somewhere, don't they?

The work shown was done through October/November of 2022. This article was originally written sometime in August and modified on 07/10/2023. If you have any thoughts, feel free to send them to me in an email. Have a nice day!
